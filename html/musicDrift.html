Racing games are a video game genre in which the player participates in a racing competition. They may be based on anything from real-world racing leagues to fantastical settings. They are distributed along a spectrum between more realistic racing simulations and more fantastical arcade-style racing games. Racing games may also fall under the category of sports video games.

At the moment, there are many analogues of "drift games" both on computers and on phones.

In the developed program was used the idea of creating a game about “drifting” with suitable music playing that will be displayed on the screen in the car, and the car itself will have almost all the functionality inherent in a real car:
  • the operation of the main headlights, dimensions and high beam headlights, the operation of the turn signals;
  • manual gear shifting.

  • W, A, S, D - car controlls
  • Space - handbrake
  • I - low beam headlights
  • K - high beam headlights
  • V - change camera
  • Q/E - indicator left/right
  • Z - indicator hazard
  • T - automativ/manual transmission
  • Left Shift - gear shift up
  • Left Ctrl - gear shist down
  • N - n gear
  • I - turn on/off engine
  • M - next audio
  • R - reload scene
  • Esc - exit game
